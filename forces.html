Centrifugal force

Centrifugal force is the force that acts on a body moving in a circular path. It is the force that keeps an object from falling straight down towards the Earth.

Free Body Diagram

A free body diagram is a visual representation of the forces acting on an object. It shows the object's weight, the forces acting on it, and the resulting acceleration. The forces acting on an object are typically the result of gravity and the forces exerted by other objects.
